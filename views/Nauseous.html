Feeling Nauseous? Dont worry.

Nausea is stomach discomfort and the sensation of wanting to vomit. Nausea can be a precursor to vomiting the contents of the stomach. The condition has many causes and can often be prevented.

Causes of Nausea

Why we think you're having Nausea

  • Stress or anxiety. .
  • Motion sickness.
  • Phobias.
  • Food poisoning.
  • Eating disorders.
  • Problems with the inner ear, such as labyrinthitis or vestibular neuritis.

    • Suggestions

    What we suggest you should do

  • Drink clear or ice-cold drinks.
  • Drink clear or ice-cold drinks.
  • Avoid fried, greasy, or sweet foods.
  • Eat slowly and eat smaller, more frequent meals.
  • Avoid activity after eating.
  • Do not mix hot and cold foods.
